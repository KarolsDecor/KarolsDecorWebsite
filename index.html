Helpful answers before you begin

Home Staging Frequently Asked Questions

What areas does Karol's Decor serve?

Karol serves Mississauga, Milton, Oakville, Burlington, Brampton, Hamilton and surrounding communities across Peel, Halton and the Hamilton area. Travel charges may apply outside the usual range.

How much does home staging cost?

Vacant staging starts at $2,000, occupied staging starts at $800, partial staging starts at $500 and consultations are $150 per hour. Final pricing depends on the property, rooms and project requirements.

What is the difference between vacant, occupied and partial staging?

Vacant staging furnishes an empty property. Occupied staging refines a home using much of the owner's furniture. Partial staging adds selected furniture, artwork, rugs or accessories where a focused update will have the strongest effect.

Does Karol personally visit each property?

Yes. Karol personally visits each property, makes the design decisions and ensures that every client receives the same attention to detail.

Is a consultation included with a staging project?

Yes. The consultation fee is included when you proceed with a staging project.

When should I contact a home stager?

Contact Karol as early as possible before photography and listing. Your target listing date helps her understand timing, availability and the right scope for the property.

Can Karol work with furniture already in my home?

Yes. Occupied and partial staging are designed to work with suitable furniture you already own, improving layout and adding selected décor or furnishings where needed.

Do you work directly with realtors?

Yes. Karol has provided residential staging services to realtors and homeowners since launching Karol's Decor in 2015.
