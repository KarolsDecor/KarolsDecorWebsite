About Me

With a background in Fine Arts Studies from OCAD University, an Interior Decorator Diploma from Sheridan College (2008), and a Home Stager Certification (HSR) (2015), I bring a unique blend of artistic creativity and professional expertise to every project. My home staging career began working for a luxury home staging company, where I developed a passion for transforming spaces into show-stopping homes.

In 2015, I launched KAROL'S DECOR Home Staging, serving realtors and homeowners in Mississauga, Brampton, Milton, Oakville, Burlington and surrounding areas. Combining a fine arts foundation with over 15 years of design experience, I create stylish, inviting interiors that connect emotionally with buyers and maximize property value. Let’s transform your property into a buyer’s dream!

Our Services & Pricing

Vacant Home Staging

Starting at $2,000 - 3 to 8 hours

Empty homes can feel cold and uninviting. We furnish and style your space with carefully chosen furniture and décor, showcasing its best features and helping buyers visualize their future.

Occupied Home Staging

Starting at $800 - 2 to 5 hours

Living in your home while selling? We’ll work with your existing furniture, rearranging and enhancing the layout to create a polished and buyer-ready look.

Partial Staging

Starting at $500 - 2 to 5 hours

Perfect for homes that need a little boost, partial staging combines your furniture with our design elements, such as artwork, rugs, and accessories, to create a cohesive and inviting atmosphere.

Consultation Services

$150/hour

In-Person Walk-Through (1–2 hours) with Report. This essential step provides expert advice on decluttering, organizing, and showcasing your home to attract buyers. With personalized recommendations and proven marketing strategies, a consultation is often all you need to get your property market-ready!

IMPORTANT NOTES

  • Consultation fees are included with any project.
  • Pricing is provided as a guideline and may vary depending on the specifics of each project.
  • Services are available in areas outside our usual range, but additional travel costs may apply.
